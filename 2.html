Rigid Lithosphere Over lies Weak Asthenosphere

According to the plate tectonics model, the crust and the uppermost, and therefore coolest, part of the mantle constitute EArth's strong outer layer, known as the lithosphere(lithos=stone,sphere=ball).

The lithosphere varies in both thickness and density, depending on whether it is oceanic lithosphere or continetal lithosphere (Figure 2.9).
